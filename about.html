About Us

KIET Tech Society is the premier technical club of the KIET Group of Institutions. We are a student-driven community dedicated to fostering innovation and technical excellence. Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements.


We have diverse domains where students can learn and grow, including: Web Development, Machine Learning (ML), Generative AI, UI/UX Design, Content Creation, Video Editing, and Photography. We provide a platform for students to collaborate on projects and enhance their skills. We are a multidisciplinary club structured into specialized domains to cater to diverse interests. Whether you are passionate about coding the future with Web Development and Machine Learning (ML), or you want to explore the cutting edge of Generative AI, we have a spot for you. We also emphasize the creative arts, fostering talent in UI/UX Design, Content Creation, Video Editing, and Photography.
